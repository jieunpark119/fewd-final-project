ABOUT

The Jangs Müller Type Foundry is established 2012 from
New Haven,CT. USA. as a branch of the Jangs Müller Design Studio to focus on Type Experiment and Research.

Jangs Müller is the principle typeface designer of this type foundry who is interested in all forms that can be converged or exchanged its shape or idea to letterform.

All fonts & products on this website can be securely purchased through PayPal. Upon payment, you will receive an e-mail containing a direct link to download your purchase and print out your invoice.

For information on web and mobile applicences, please contact us. All fonts are provided in OpenType format for Mac & PC.
